# Vespera Systems Company Structure

## Brand Architecture

- `vesperasystems.com` is the company site for Vespera Systems.
- `vespera.systems` is the flagship product surface.
- Both domains share the same Vespera visual system: monochrome field, graph-theory geometry, thin-line dividers, restrained motion, and the Konigsberg-inspired mark.

## Visual Direction

- Black-field background with white geometry and subtle grey hierarchy.
- Use the graph-theory mark and wordmark lockups from the brand guide.
- Prefer editorial pacing over dashboard density on the company site.
- Keep motion sparse and structural on the company site; keep motion reactive and operational on the product site.
- Replace "map" language with "graph" where it refers to the system or the core display.

## vesperasystems.com

### Purpose

Present Vespera Systems as a quant finance technology company with a clear point of view, institutional credibility, and a visible flagship product.

### Recommended sitemap

1. `/`
   - Hero
   - What We Are
   - What We Build
   - Product preview
   - Research / systems thinking
   - Careers signal
   - Contact
2. `/about`
   - Company thesis
   - Why graph thinking matters in markets
   - How Vespera works across quant, legal, AI, and systems engineering
3. `/research`
   - Essays, notes, market systems observations, graph-driven thinking
4. `/careers`
   - Roles, principles, team expectations, technical culture
5. `/contact`
   - Institutional introductions, hiring, partnerships

### Homepage section structure

1. Hero
   - Headline focused on Vespera Systems as a company
   - Subhead framing finance as a graph of capital, people, incentives, and time
   - CTA to contact the company
   - Secondary CTA to enter `vespera.systems`
2. What We Are
   - Quant finance technology company
   - Research-led
   - Graph-first worldview
3. What We Build
   - Legal intelligence
   - Conversational systems
   - Agentic workflows
   - Graph-native market interfaces
4. Product
   - `vespera.systems` as the flagship product
   - One or two stills or a restrained live preview
5. Research
   - Short editorial blocks on systems thinking, graph theory, market structure
6. Careers
   - Lean recruiting invitation, not a generic startup pitch
7. Contact
   - Direct and minimal

## vespera.systems

### Purpose

Operate as the flagship product and demo environment.

### Recommended product routes

1. `/`
   - Full-screen graph display
2. `/graph`
   - Optional explicit graph route if needed later
3. `/legal`
   - Legal intelligence surface
4. `/chat`
   - Conversational analysis surface
5. `/agents`
   - Agent workflows and orchestration
6. `/config`
   - Operator configuration
7. `/admin`
   - Estate management

### Product principles

- The graph is the central visual and conceptual object.
- Product features should feel like instruments attached to the graph.
- Labels, interfaces, and workflows should reinforce relationships, flows, and structure.
- Avoid generic SaaS patterns and KPI-box thinking.
